How the maximum potential withhold is calculated

The calculation compares delivered activity so far (with any adjustments) to the full-year contracted activity.

  1. Adjusted delivered activity at mid-year (30 Sept), including any carry forward or amendments, is doubled to estimate full-year delivery.
  2. The estimated full-year delivery is subtracted from the total contracted activity.
  3. The result is multiplied by the UDA rate to give the maximum withhold amount.
